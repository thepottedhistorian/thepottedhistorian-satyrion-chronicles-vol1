# The Satyrion Chronicles, Vol. I
## A Biography of a Species: *Orchis mascula*

> *"Tracing the ancient roots of a modern collection."*

**Project:** [The Potted Historian](https://thepottedhistorian.com)  
**Status:** In Progress - Digital Scriptorium

## About this Volume
*The Satyrion Chronicles, Volume I* serves as a comprehensive biography of *Orchis mascula* (the Early Purple Orchid). This research volume explores the intersection of pre-17th-century botanical folklore, early modern herbalism, and the eventual scientific classification of the species. 

From the "Satyrion" of Dioscorides to the woodcuts of the Renaissance, this work archives the cultural and botanical evolution of one of history's most storied orchids.

## Archival Contents
This repository contains high-fidelity digital plates sourced from:
* **Theophrastus**, *De Historia Plantarum* (Amsterdam, 1644 edition, ed. Bodaeus à Stapel).
* **Jan Berntsz**, *Den groten herbarius* (Utrecht, 1538).
* **Leonhart Fuchs**, *De Historia Stirpium* (Basel, 1542).
* **Pedanius Dioscorides**, *De Materia Medica* (1st Century).
